About Me

Water and land don't stay still — especially in Bangladesh's deltas. That's the question driving most of my work: how do coastlines, wetlands, and aquifers change, and what does that mean for the people who depend on them?
I'm an M.Sc. candidate in Water Resources Development at BUET's IWFM, with a civil engineering background and a growing toolkit in geospatial analysis — Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS, Random Forest classification, RockWorks. Here, you'll find the research, scripts, and analyses that came out of chasing that question.
